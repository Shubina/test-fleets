Our Mission

With over 1.3M deaths each year worldwide, we can’t afford to wait for autonomous vehicles
to become mainstream and remove human error.

Nexar aims to rid the world of car crashes, now. To do so, we use cutting edge technology to build the world’s first open vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) network that connects drivers on the road and stops collisions with real-time alerts.

Our V2V network reduces human error and improves safety in today’s cars, while providing the basis for the self-driving cars of the future. If you are passionate about road safety and driven toward success, come join us on our journey to make the world safer.

Our technology

Nexar leverages machine vision and sensor fusion algorithms with each driver’s smartphone to understand a car’s surroundings and both predict and prevent collisions. We connect all drivers’ phones in the world’s first vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) network that analyzes dangerous driving patterns and warns all drivers when dangers are ahead.

With our enormous and growing user base of drivers from dozens of countries, we understand the roads better than just about anyone else.
